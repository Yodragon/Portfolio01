    Artist Statement

    For much of my life, art had manifested itself in the physical world as pencil drawings on paper with the occasional crayon colorings.
    It wasn’t until I reached middle school that a teacher enraptured by new tech had introduced me to the world of digital art creation.
    And now what was once tangible became intangible, the art finding life in a new world separate from ours.
    Just like that middle school teacher, I too was entranced by the new possibilities that this digital realm promised.
    And I have remained in that realm ever since.

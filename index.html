    Twinkle in His Eye

    I wanted to test out creating a simple GIF animation using Photoshop.
    Since GIFs have limited color ranges, I thought about playing around with that in mind.
    So not just strong colors in key areas of the image, but also the lack of color as well.

    Program: Adobe Photoshop

    Space Kitty Sandwich

    For this piece, I wanted to practice my photo manipulation skills.
    It was fun to combine cartoon-like elements with realistic elements.
    As a cat owner, I often see cat loafs so a sandwich fits the bill perfectly.
    There's also nothing more fun than adventures in space with rainbows and sparkles of course.

    Program: Adobe Photoshop

    Bass in Black

    This is a tribute to one of the most influential graphic designers in Hollywood history, Saul Bass.
    I wanted to play around with geometry and jagged linework in this illustration.
    I was also inspired by old spy movie posters, emphasizing a strong black and white figure contrasted by a sharp red background.

    Program: Adobe Illustrator
